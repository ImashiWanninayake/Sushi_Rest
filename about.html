Our Story

Welcome to our family-style restaurants, where families and friends of all ages can relish the joy of dining out. Our mission is to ensure that everyone in your group finds their own favourite dishes and leaves with contented hearts and full bellies. We understand the importance of creating cherished memories, and that is why we wholeheartedly embrace the essence of good times, great food at a value, and guarantee your satisfaction from start to finish. Come dine with us and experience the pure delight of leaving not just satisfied but gratified.



All-you-can-eat

Our ALL YOU CAN EAT menu means you can try anything, try everything and leave feeling full and happy for only $39.80* per person. Like a doting grandmother, we want to feed you with food that makes everyone in your group happy. Over 30 mouthwatering dishes, each crafted fresh with every order.
When you visit one of our restaurants (50 and counting) our friendly team will quickly bring you any of the items you order from our menu. We tailor the portion of each dish as per the group size, so everyone can try more and won’t be stuffed by a few dishes.



Milestone

2013
In 2013, Okami opened its first store in Colombo, which marked the beginning of our journey.
2015
Two years later, in 2015, SUSHI opened our second store in Kandy, which helped to solidify our presence in the market.
2016
From 2016 to 2019, SUSHI continued to expand its operations and opened a total of 18 stores across in Colombo. This period of growth helped SUSHI to establish ourselves as a leading Japanese All You Can Eat brand.
2020
In 2020, despite the challenges posed by the pandemic, SUSHI opened 10 new stores, including the first location in Sydney. This expansion was a testament to our resilience and determination to continue serving our customers even in difficult times
2021
The year 2021 witnessed SUSHI opens its first store in the ACT and expands into regional New Gall, bringing the affordable Japanese feast to more customers across the country.
2022
In 2022, SUSHI launched in Adelaide, adding another city to our growing list of locations
2023
As SUSHI looks forward to its 10th year in 2023, we are excited to announce that we are launching to Brisbane, further expanding our footprint across Australia.
